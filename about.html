About

Why Calc-HQ exists.

Calc-HQ is the authority layer for a calculator network focused on finance, payroll timing, business-day logic, banking cutoffs, and compensation questions. Instead of acting like a thin directory, the hub groups tools by problem type and gives visitors context before they click into a specific calculator.

The site is intentionally simple: no accounts, no login requirements, and no unnecessary complexity.
